6.01  GENERAL

This chapter provides information on dictated letters and BDN (Benefit Delivery Network) generated letters for Chapter 32 and section 903 cases.  BDN generates letters for most Chapter 32 awards, terminations, disallowances, and master records requiring certifications.  For BDN development letters which apply to Chapter 32 as well as other benefits, see part II, Chapter 7.  For development letters which apply only to Chapter 32, see paragraph 6.13 of this chapter.

6.03  BDN GENERATED LETTERS

a.  Copies.  The original of a generated letter is mailed to the payee and a copy, if provided, is sent to the RO (regional office) for dropfiling in the claims folder.  If a valid power of attorney has been established in the Chapter 32 or section 903 master record, the appropriate code will be shown at the top of the letter below the regional office address.  One additional copy will be sent to the RO for release to the holder of the power of attorney.

b.  Suppression of Generated Letters

(1)  Suppress the computer generated letter and send a dictated letter in the following cases:

(i)  If the generated letter will not be completely informative; or

(ii)  If a special advance payment is authorized.

(2)  If a generated letter is to be suppressed on BDN, the LETTER SUPPRESSOR indicator must be checked on the 501 Authorization screen for award actions and on the 406 Stop/Suspend/Resume screen for terminations.

(3)  If a letter cannot be generated and the letter is not suppressed, the RO will receive notice that a letter was not generated.  Upon receipt of the notice, obtain the claims folder for review.  If a dictated letter was issued, dispose of the message without further action.  If a dictated letter was not issued, send one to the claimant.

c.  Appeal Paragraph.  Each generated letter, other than notices of delinquent certification or development action, will contain a detailed explanation of how a claimant can appeal a decision.  If a generated letter is suppressed which would have included an appeal paragraph, VA (Veterans Affairs) Form 1-4107, Notice of Procedural and Appellate Rights, must be attached to the dictated letter.

d.  Production.  BDN generated letters are produced at the Hines BDC (Benefits Delivery Center) after cycle processing and are printed in uppercase letters.
