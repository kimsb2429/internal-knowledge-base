14.01 GENERAL

This chapter contains three types of tables.
 

a. Training Time Equivalency Table (paragraph 14.02a, paragraph 14.02b, paragraph 14.02c, and paragraph 14.02d)). This table is used to determine the training time when the student is enrolled in a nonstandard term. See chapter 6 for details.

b. Rate Based on Total Charges Table (paragraph 14.03). Occasionally, Target (BDN) cannot compute a rate which is based on the total charges of the student's course. When this happens, Adjudication must use this table to manually compute the monthly rate.

c. Entitlement Charging and Conversion Tables (paragraph 14.04, paragraph 14.05, and paragraph 14.06). Occasionally, a manual computation of entitlement is necessary. This is most frequently the case when an award is processed outside the Target system. Use these tables for charging chapter 35 entitlement except in correspondence cases. For chapter 35 correspondence cases, see Part 7: Chapter 4 - Award Processing and Other Authorization Issues. Use these tables for chapters 30, 32, and 1606 cases except for correspondence, flight, apprenticeship/On-the-Job Training (OJT), and cooperative training. For the latter types of training, see Part 7: Chapter 4 - Award Processing and Other Authorization Issues of the appropriate benefit part

14.03. TABLE FOR COMPUTING RATE BASED ON TOTAL CHARGES

USE THE TABLE: Multiply the established charges for tuition and fees by the factor shown below for the certified period of enrollment. Compare the resultant sum with the amount payable for the number of hours enrolled. The rates are shown in chapter 1 of the appropriate benefit part (pt. V, VI, VII or VIII). Authorize the lesser of the two rates. Round all fractions to the nearest cent. Chapter 35 Example: Established charges (tuition and fees) - $150. Certified period of enrollment - 4 months, 12 days. Number of hours enrolled - 5 semester hours. Rate Table - $202. Table below - $150 X .2273 = $34.0950 or $34.10. Rate of Payment is the lesser of the two amounts ($202 or $34.10). Pay $34.10.
