Sub-Chapter 10. Tutorial Assistance Program

Sub-Chapter 10. Tutorial Assistance Program

An individual who is eligible to receive benefits under chapter 33 may receive additional assistance for tutorial services. VA will pay for this assistance if tutoring is necessary for the student to successfully complete his or her program of education.  Tutorial assistance helps students pay for necessary tutoring and is a supplement to the student’s regular education benefit.

a. Benefit Payment Details

The monthly rate of tutorial assistance is the lesser of the cost of tutoring or $100. The maximum amount payable for tutorial assistance is $1,200.

b. Requirements

All of the following criteria must be met for a student to be eligible for tutorial assistance.

  • The student must be in a post secondary program ½-time or more. For Chapter 33, the Rate of Pursuit must be at least .50.
  • The student must have a deficiency in a course that is part of his or her approved program.
  • The student must be enrolled in the course during the quarter, semester, or term in which the tutoring is received for the course. Tutoring may not occur between quarters or semesters.

c. Application

The VA student, tutor, School Certifying Official (SCO), and professor/instructor [when tutorial assistance under the Post-9/11 GI Bill] must complete a VA Form 22-1990t, Application and Enrollment Certification for Individualized Tutorial Assistance.
