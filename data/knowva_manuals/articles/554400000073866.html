7.01 Search for Claimant

a. Search

You will start a claim for a ToE recipient the same way you would for a regular claim: by searching Chapter 33 records and VADIR.

The following scenario is a Transfer of Entitlement (ToE) claim, which will demonstrate that a veteran is transferring 15 months of Chapter 33 benefits to a dependent.

When you receive VA Form 22-1990e, the ToE application form, use the Search Chapter 33 screen to search for the claimant.

Enter the claimant’s SSN and select the Search Chapter 33 button.

b. No Results

If no records are found after searching the CH33 database, the No Results Found message will be displayed on the Search Results of Chapter 33 screen, and the Search VADIR button becomes available on the far right of the screen.

Continue the claim by searching the VADIR database. Click the Search VADIR button.

c. Create New Record

If no VADIR record is found for the claimant, click the Create New Chapter 33 Record button to continue the claim. 

In our example, no VADIR record is found for the claimant.

7.02 Claimant Biography

a. The Claimant Biography screen will be autofilled if a claimant’s record is in VADIR. If there is no record in VADIR, you will need to manually enter the claimant’s biographical information.

On the Claimant Biography screen, enter all the required data as you would with a regular claim. Then click the Next button to save the record and continue the claim. Remember to click the Next button to save. If you click the Previous button or try to navigate back before saving, your changes will be lost.

Click the Next button to save, and go to the Work Product History screen.

7.03 Work Product History

a. Add Work Product

New claimants will not have any Work Products at this time. Click the Add Work Product button on the right-hand side of the screen.

You will be taken to the Entitlement Source screen.

 

b. Entitlement Source

Entitlement Source refers to the period of service from which the entitlement will be drawn. On the Entitlement Source screen, the user selects the source for Chapter 33 benefit:

  • Claimant’s own service (SELF)
  • Fry Scholarship (FRY)
  • Transfer of Entitlement (ToE)

c. ToE will only be available as an Entitlement Source if the transferor (Clark Kent) has already transferred entitlement to Jenny Kent in the LTS.

Note: In cases where there is no evidence of the Transferor being approved for ToE, you should select Source Not Listed to disallow the claim.

In our example, the transferor has been approved to transfer entitlement to a dependent. Click Search from the Tasklist on the left-hand menu, and search for the transferor’s record to transfer out entitlement.

7.04 Transfer-Out Entitlement

To transfer out entitlement, you must locate the record of the person transferring his or her entitlement. To do this, go back to the Search screen by using the Tasklist on the left-hand menu.

a. Search for Transferor

Begin your search by entering the transferor’s SSN. Click the Search Chapter 33 button. Again, if no results are found, you will need to search VADIR.

If no VADIR results are found and transferor’s SSN was properly entered, click the Continue Without Association button.

b. Transferor’s Work Product History

Because the transferor has a CH33 record, you will navigate past the Claimant Biography screen. You have the option of navigating back to that screen if updates are required.

The transferor has an Authorized Work Product, and you will need to create a new one to transfer out entitlement. Click the Add Work Product button to continue.

c. On the Entitlement Source screen, select Self as the entitlement source by clicking the Select button next to the Self source.

d. On the Work Product Creation screen, enter the required data for the Supplemental Award. Then click the Create Work Product button to continue the claim.

Learn more about the data entry rules for the Work Product History LTS Screen

 e. Transferor Entitlement and Kickers

The Entitlement and Kickers screen has the Entitlement Transferred Out section and the Add Transferee button.

 

Click the Add Transferee button to continue the claim.

f. When you click on the Add Transferee button, the Transfer Out Entitlement pop-up window will appear. 

g. The Transfer Out Entitlement pop-up window displays the following information:

  • SSN: The dependent to which the veteran is transferring his entitlement.
  • Months of Chapter 33 Entitlement: This must be equal to or less than the Transferor’s remaining entitlement.
  • Begin Date: This is the date that transferred entitlement is available to the dependent, and entry is required.
  • End Date: This is the date entitlement transfer is revoked and allows entry of a future date, and it is not required.
  • Chapter 30 Kicker: This must be equal to or less than the Transferor’s remaining Chapter 30 kicker.
  • Chapter 1606 Kicker: This must be equal to or less than Transferor’s remaining Chapter 1606 kicker.
  • Relationship: Dropdown options are Spouse and Child.

The fields with an asterisk  are required. They are:

  • SSN
  • Months of Chapter 33 Entitlement,
  • Begin Date
  • Relationship

Note: The LTS requires that the dependent or transferee record must exist before transferring out entitlement. Click the Save button to continue the claim.

You will return back to the Entitlement and Kickers screen, which is now autofilled with the information you entered. The Edit button is now enabled. This allows you to modify all transfer information except SSN.

Click the Next button to save and continue the claim.

h. Transferor’s Enrollment

If the transferor has additional enrollment data to enter, enter the required data here. In our example, the transferor has no additional Enrollment Data to enter. Click the Next button to continue the claim.

Learn more about the data entry rules for the Enrollment LTS Screen 

i. Transferor’s Work Product Summary

Review each section of the Work Product Summary and authorize the record.

Click the Search link in the Task List on the left-hand menu to return to Jenny’s record.

7.05 Continue Transfer of Entitlement Claim

a. Enter SSN

Continue Jenny’s claim by entering her SSN. Click the Search Chapter 33 button to begin.

b. Click the Continue Without Association button.

c. You will navigate to the Work Product History screen. Click the Add Work Product button to continue to the Entitlement Source screen.

d. On the Entitlement Source screen, select ToE as the entitlement source by clicking the Select button next to the ToE source.

e. You will navigate to the Prior VA Training screen. Enter any Entitlement Used under (non-CH33) benefits by clicking the Add Entitlement button. In our example, the dependent has not used any benefits under other non-CH33 programs. Click the Next button to continue to the Enrollment screen.

7.06 Transferee Enrollment

a. Under 18

If a child is under the age of 18, you must enter a date for High School Graduation or Equivalent in the Miscellaneous section. This is a section on the Enrollment screen for ToE claims only. 

b. Click the Add Enrollment button to continue the claim.

With the Add Enrollment pop-up window displayed, enter all the required fields. Then click the Save button to continue.

c. The Eligibility and Entitlement banner has been updated along with the Enrollment section.

d. Click the Next button to continue the claim.
