Sub-Chapter 5. Entitlement and Kickers - LTS Data Entry Rules

This section describes data entry rules for the Entitlement and Kickers screen in the Long Term Solution (LTS) system. You will find data entry rules to help you with the following:

5.01 Entitlement Used

5.02 Benefit Relinquished

5.03 Chapter 33 Start

5.04 Kickers & Supplementals

5.05 Entitlement Transferred Out

5.06 Comments

 

NOTE: In general, individuals are entitled to 36 months of Chapter 33 benefits. Exceptions are as follows:

  • Chapter 33 entitlement is limited to remaining Chapter 30 entitlement if Chapter 30 is relinquished and entitlement was used previously.
  • Individuals who are entitled to more than one benefit may only receive a maximum of 48 months of combined benefits.

You will notice the Eligibility and Entitlement banner at the top of the screen. The banner cannot be edited. It is for information purposes only. All fields are prepopulated with input from previous screens.

5.01 Entitlement Used

a. Start with the Entitlement Used section and click the Add Entitlement button. This will display the Add/Edit Entitlement Used pop-up window.

Once data have been entered and you have saved the required data, you will be able to edit or delete the entitlement. 

 

b. Add/Edit Entitlement Used Pop-up Window

The Add/Edit Entitlement Used pop-up window allows you to select the Type of entitlement and enter the amount of Entitlement Used. Both fields are required.

You should input all non-Chapter 33 benefits under which the claimant has used entitlement. You must also enter any benefit that is to be relinquished, even if the entitlement used is zero.

The Type drop-down list has the following options to choose from:

  • Chapter 1606
  • Chapter 1607
  • Chapter 30
  • Chapter 31
  • Chapter 32
  • Chapter 34
  • Chapter 35
  • NCS

In the example below, the claimant has used 16 months and 05 days of Chapter 30 entitlement. He is eligible at the time of application for CH33 benefits.

 

In this case, you will choose Chapter 30 from the Type drop-down box. In the Entitlement Used field you will enter 16-05.

Entitlement Used should be entered as MM-DD.

If the claimant is eligible for Chapter 30, 1606, or 1607 at the time of CH33 election, the checkbox must be selected.

Click the Save button to continue and return to the Entitlement and Kickers screen and to continue.

The Entitlement Used section will be updated to include the previous entry. You can make another entry if necessary.

To make another entry, click the Add Entitlement button again to add a second benefit.

If you are done adding entitlements, move to the Benefit Relinquished section.

 5.02 Benefit Relinquished

a. The Benefit Relinquished section allows you the option to Add/Edit Benefit Relinquished so the claimant can use Chapter 33 entitlement. This is required if the claimant is eligible and must relinquish a benefit under Chapter 30, 1606, or 1607.

b. Click the Add Benefit Relinquished button to activate the Add/Edit Benefit Relinquished pop-up window.

Add/Edit Benefit Relinquished Pop-up Window

In this pop-up window, enter data for any other education benefits a claimant is relinquishing. Fill out the following required fields:

  • Election Date (MM/DD/YYYY)
  • Benefit Relinquished (drop-down box)

In this example, your only option for the Benefit Relinquished drop-down is Chapter 30 based on the entitlement added for this claimant during the Add Entitlement process.

If you are selecting Chapter 30, you must also enter the Remaining Entitlement, which is the amount of months and days of remaining entitlement. Enter the Chapter 30 Remaining entitlement with MM/DD formatting.

Users can add a date in the future (meaning, after today’s date).

5.03 Chapter 33 Start

a. Chapter 33 Start is not a required field. You may enter an optional Chapter 33 Start Date if the individual wants to begin receiving benefits on a specific date not corresponding with and AFTER the Election Date. If the claimant has an Election Date, the Chapter 33 Start Date will default to that value but may be changed to a later date.

If you enter a Start Date, it must be formatted as MM/DD/YYYY.

Click the Submit button to store the value if you enter or modify the date.

 

 

5.04 Kickers & Supplementals

a. The Kickers & Supplementals section allows you to add or edit Kickers and Supplemental education assistance. 

 

Select the Add Kicker/Supplemental button to activate the Add/Edit Kicker/Supplemental pop-up window.

In the pop-up window the following fields will be displayed:

  • Type *
  • $/Month *
  • Start Date *
  • End Date
  • Initial *

Those with an asterisk * are required.

b. Type is a drop-down list with the following options:

  • Chapter 1606 Kicker
  • Chapter 30 Kicker
  • Chapter 33 Kicker
  • Chapter 33 Supplemental

c. $/Month is the dollar amount per month for full-time training. The field will accept up to 7 characters.

d. Start Date reflects the date the Kicker is available on or after August 1, 2009.

e. End Date reflects the termination date of the kicker. The Kicker End Date must be equal to or greater than the Start Date. This is not a required field. Generally a kicker should never change however, an End Date may be entered when a kicker must be corrected for point forward adjustments.

f. Initial refers to the initial entitlement at the time of the application for Chapter 33 and may not be greater than 36-00 (36 months). 

 

Click the Save button to continue and return to the Entitlement and Kickers screen.

5.05 Deleting Entitlement and Kickers

a. To delete an Entitlement Used or Kicker/Supplemental, you must place a check in the box next to the item you want to delete. 

When you check the box next to Chapter 33 Kicker, the Delete button is enabled.

Click the Delete button to display the Delete Confirmation pop-up window. Select the Delete button to delete the record and return to the previous screen.

 

5.06 Entitlement Transferred Out

a. The Entitlement Transferred Out section is used to set aside entitlement that has been transferred out to a qualifying dependent. See details about this in the ToE section of the manual.
