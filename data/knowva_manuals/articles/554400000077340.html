SUBCHAPTER II.  CHAPTER 30 DOD MASTER RECORD INQUIRY

4.10  CHAPTER 30 DOD DATA RECORD SCREEN (30D)

a.  General.  There are two very distinct Chapter 30 DOD Data Record screens as described below.

(1)  ["Regular" 30D Screens.  These "regular" 30D screens are for all persons who meet the different chapter 30 category criteria defined in part V.  (Figure 4.15 shows a 30D screen for a category I claimant.  This could be a regular 30D screen for any eligibility category.)  If any fields on the 30D screen are blank, it means DOD does not have the data in its chapter 30 database (the data may exist elsewhere in DOD).

(2)  "Special" 30D Screens.  These "special" 30D screens are limited to those individuals who were eligible to receive chapter 34 benefits, the Vietnam Era GI Bill, on December 31, 1989.  These "special" 30D screens for category II cases were created by VA (NOT DOD) based on data present during 1989.  These "special" 30D screens can not show any data other than that data present when these screens were created during 1989.  See figure 4.16 for a category II 30D screen.

NOTE:  There is also a Chapter 1606] DOD Data Record, commonly referred to as the "DOD" screen.  See Training Guide 22-99-5, The Chapter 1606 User Guide, part II, chapter 1 for details.] 

b.  Inquiry Processing

(1)  Restrictions.  The 30D screen is intended for use by all [ROs in resolving inquiries.  It is for use by RPOs in making eligibility determinations.  The 30D screen is NOT a final determination of eligibility.  ONLY THE RPOs may make an eligibility determination.  No VA personnel should make definitive statements as to eligibility, rates, or monthly kickers unless the RPO has actually processed a claim and BDN shows a master record (i.e., M21, M22 and M23 screens).  Inquiries that cannot be answered with the use of BDN] must continue to be referred to the RPO.

(2)  Ready Screen entries.  Make the following entries on the Ready screen to obtain a 30D screen:

(a)  Command MINQ.

(b)  Operator's password.

c.  Updating the DOD Record

(1)  VA personnel cannot update the 30D screen.  The screens are only accessible for inquiry processing.

(2)  [DOD can update all fields of the regular 30D screen during the weekly update processing.  BDN will generate an NOE (Notice of Exception) that explains action taken when a DOD record is updated or added and a matching BDN benefit master record exists.  See  Part 5: Chapter 5 - Notice of Exception Processing for chapter 30 NOES (Notice of Exception Subsystem) processing instructions.  

(3)  The last update of the "special" 30D screen was on November 7, 1989.  BDN will generate an NOE message if this screen is ever updated by DOD in the future.  BDN also generates NOE messages when the DOD record is updated as the result of a change in the chapter 34 record.  See Part 5: Chapter 5 - Notice of Exception Processing for chapter 30 NOES processing instructions.]

d.  Multiple DoD Records 

(1)  [Multiple 30D screens are possible.  If there is more than one 30D screen, the RPO will review each screen to determine which, if any, is the proper one to establish eligibility.  Records are deleted from DOD's database only if they were erroneously established.

(2)  If there is more than one DoD record for a chapter 30 claimant, BDN] shows a separate DOD (chapter 1606)or 30D screen for each DOD record (to a maximum of 20).  The first record on the DOD screen shows "MULTIPLE DOD RECORDS - HIT ENTER TO CONTINUE."  The first record on the 30D screen shows "MULTIPLE 30D RECORDS - HIT ENTER TO CONTINUE."  "ALL RECORDS HAVE BEEN REVIEWED" shows on the message line of the last DOD or 30D record.  Entering RES in NEXT SCREEN shows the first record.

(3)  BDN shows "CH 30 RECORD (30D) EXISTS" when the chapter 1606] DOD screen is being viewed and a chapter 30 eligibility record exists.  Enter 30D in NEXT SCREEN field to review the chapter 30 record.

NOTE:  This is true only if the operator is reviewing an "old" DOD screen.  The old "DOD" screen is the DOD screen in effect before the publication of TG 22-99-5.

(4)  BDN shows "CH 106 RECORD (DOD) EXISTS" if the chapter 30 30D screen is being viewed and a chapter 1606 eligibility record exists.  Enter DOD in NEXT SCREEN field to review the chapter 1606 eligibility record.

NOTE:  This information refers to an "old" DOD screen.  To obtain a current DOD screen, use the MINQ command with "DOD in the Screen Number field and"1606" in the Benefit field.] 

e.  Service Data.  [Regular 30D screens can show service data (Branch, EOD, RAD, Separation Reason, Character of Service, Enlistment Term, and Education Level) in two columns.  Special 30D screens can show service data in four columns.

(1)  Regular 30D screens show the service data currently in DOD's chapter 30 database.

(2)  Special 30D screens DO NOT SHOW current service information.  The first three columns show BIRLS data as of February 4, 1989.  The fourth column shows DOD service data as of November 7, 1989.  The fourth column EOD is a BASD (Basic Active Service Date).  This is an adjustable date and does not always reflect the date entered on duty.]
