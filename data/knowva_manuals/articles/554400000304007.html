Appendix A.  Active DGI Work Product Summary (WPS) Screen Messages

Change Date           June 30, 2025

Describes the active messages that may display on the DGI Work Product Summary (WPS) screen for original and supplemental chapter 33 claims.

Automation Messages for Original Claims

Message Text

Scenario

Action Needed

Claim already in progress

Used to offramp claims that already have work pending.

Process the claim following standard guidance.

TOE family member

already in progress

Used to offramp claims that have a family member with work pending.

Process the TOE claim following standard guidance.

CH33 claim exists

Used when a Chapter 33 claim already exists for the beneficiary.

Process the claim following standard guidance.

Claimant has TOE data

in VADIR

Used when VADIR shows the Veteran potentially has transferred entitlement to dependents.

Ensure that TOE information in VIS is properly reviewed.

Claimant has used

CH30 entitlement

Used when Chapter 30 entitlement was previously used.

Ensure that used Chapter 30 entitlement is accounted for.  Take appropriate relinquishment actions, if applicable.

An eMPWR payment error

exists

Used when a payment was returned in error status or if the claimant is in a suspended status.

Follow instructions for handling payments in error (M22-4, Part II, 6.A) or payments in suspense (M22-4, Part II, 2.D.4).

Validation error(s) detected

during automated claim

Used when a rules error is returned during automation.

Process the claim following standard guidance.  Consult with coach if error persists.

Disallowance 17 letter for

TOE claimant produced

Used when a Dis17 letter is produced for a TOE claim.

Process the TOE claim following standard guidance.

No Letters Produced

Used when no letters are produced.

Review the claim to confirm if no letter being sent is appropriate.  If not, a letter may need to be created manually.

Stop Automation box

checked

Used when the Stop Automation box on the Bio screen is checked.

Review the stop automation reason before processing.

VACO Stop Automation

Used when VACO has a stop automation marked for beneficiary.

Review the stop automation reason before processing.

Entitlement source

does not exist

Used when no such entitlement source exists for a claimant.

Process the claim following standard guidance.

Character of Service is

not Honorable

Used when VADIR indicates a character of service of not honorable.

Review the file for eligible periods of service/re-enlistments

Net Award generated

before any enrollment or

other action taken in the

claim

Used when an award results without any action taken on the claim.

Review the awards and determine why a net award is being generated.  Take appropriate steps to resolve.

Claimant is under 18 at the

time of claim and has no

high school graduation date

Used when claimant is under 18 and no high school graduation date is provided.

Review the application for a graduation date.  Modify the original letter, as appropriate, before sending out certificate of eligibility.

Debts outside amendment

process exist on claim

Used when debts exist other than those caused by an amendment.

Review the claim to determine the source of the debt.  Take appropriate adjudicative steps.

A change in VADIR

transfer of entitlement data

has been detected.

Used when VADIR shows the Veteran has made a change to entitlement transferred to dependents.

Ensure that TOE information in VIS is properly reviewed.

Big Pay

Used when DGI detects a potential Big Pay scenario.

Carefully review all payments to determine if Big Pay threshold is met.  Follow Big Pay procedures.

Older claim(s) cannot be

authorized with new claims

received at time of

automated processing

Used when old enrollments are in the Enrollment Manager Inbox.

Review the old enrollments to determine if they are payable and what adjudicative action is appropriate.  Develop as necessary.

CH30 or 1606 Kicker Exists

Used when VADIR indicates a kicker exists.

Review VIS and other sources for kicker information.

Unable to relinquish other

chapter benefits

Used when a beneficiary is eligible for other benefits and an error in relinquishment occurs.

Manually relinquish another benefit, as appropriate, following standard procedures.

CH30 has 0 months of

remaining entitlement

Used a beneficiary has no Chapter 30 entitlement remaining.

Review Chapter 30 entitlement use and appropriately account for entitlement used in DGI.

Claimant has excluded

periods in VADIR

Used when a beneficiary has periods of excluded service in VADIR.

Carefully review service history in VADIR.  Possible scenario requiring development.

Claimant has Marine Corps

Reserve GRAS training

period and less than 30

months of creditable service

Used when the beneficiary has an MCR GRAS training period and less than 90% benefit level.

Follow MCR GRAS training period procedures.

Claimants disagrees with

service data from VADIR

Used when the beneficiary indicates they disagree with their service data on file.

Thoroughly review service.  Potential for development.
