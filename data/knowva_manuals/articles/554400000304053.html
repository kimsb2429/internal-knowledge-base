Appendix B.  Possible Business Transaction (BT) Fields in eMPWR

Change Date           June 30, 2025

This appendix describes the possible business transaction fields and values in the Enterprise Management of Payment Workload and Reporting (eMPWR) for Chapter 33 payments.

Transaction Field

Value

jrnLctnId

Shows the Regional Processing Office (RPO) of the authorizer.

  • “282” for automatically authorized business transactions (BTs)
  • “307” for BTs authorized by the Buffalo RPO
  • “351” for BTs authorized by the Muskogee RPO

jrnUserId

Shows the user ID of the authorizer.

externalBusinessTranID

Shows a unique tracking identification for each BT.

businessTranId

Shows a unique identification for each BT.

businessTranNumber

Shows the number of the BT.

businessTranTypeCode

Shows the type of BT.

  • “96R” for Chapter 30 Contribution Refund
  • “06F” for Rural Relocation
  • “06I” for Monthly Housing Allowance (MHA) or Kickers

o   MHA and Kicker payments are distinguished by different businessTranNumber values.

  • “06L” for Work Study
  • “06N” for National Exam
  • “06O” for Books and Supplies
  • “06P” for Licensing and Certification
  • “06S” for Tuition and Fees
  • “06T” for Tutorial Assistance
  • “06Y” for Yellow Ribbon

financialBusinessTranAmt

Shows the sum of subsidiaryTranAmt records with the same businessTranId

locationId

Shows the RPO of jurisdiction.

  • “307” for BTs authorized by the Buffalo RPO.
  • “351” for BTs authorized by the Muskogee RPO.

programTypeCode

Shows “CH33” for all Chapter 33 claims.

statusTypeCode

Shows “AUTH” for all Chapter 33 claims.

subsidiaryTranAmt

Shows the amount of the payment or receivable for the BT.

transactionDate

Shows the date of authorization of the BT (when the work product is authorized).

auditRelatedInd

N/A

awardLineReasonTypeCode

Required for all BTs.

  • “OTH” is used for reasons not otherwise identified.
  • “00” is used for first awards at either an Institute of High Learning (IHL) or a Non-College Degree (NCD) institution.
  • “60” is used for subsequent awards after a first award has been authorized.
  • “65” is used when a beneficiary’s entitlement exhausts during the given award period.
  • “70” is used when terms overlap or when amendments and/or corrections have been made to a previously paid enrollment.
  • “DB” is used for any positive or negative awards related to the death of a beneficiary.

awardTypeCode

Shows the type of the award.

  • “30RFND” for Chapter 30 Contribution Refund
  • “BOOKS” for Books and Supplies
  • “HOUSE” for Monthly Housing Allowance
  • “KICK” for Kickers
  • “LAC” for Licensing and Certification
  • “NE” for National Exam
  • “RURAL” for Rural Relocation
  • “TA” for Tutorial Assistance
  • “TF” for Tuition and Fees
  • “WS” for Work study
  • “YR” for Yellow Ribbon

discoveryDate

Shows for negative award values (receivables) only.  Provides the date of the authorization of the receivable.

effectiveDate

Shows the first day of the payable period.  Equivalent to a “Start Pay Date.”

endDate

Shows the last day of the payable period.  Equivalent to an “End Pay Date.”

externalRefDate

N/A

externalRefNumber

N/A

fileNumber

Shows the file number from DGI.

ledgerAccountTypeCode

Shows the cost code field.  See M22-4, Part II, 2.D.14.j for cost code information.

ledgerTypeCode

Shows “COST” for all Chapter 33 claims.

payeeTypeCode

Shows the relationship between beneficiary and Veteran.

  • “00” for Veteran/serviceperson
  • “10” for spouse of Veteran/serviceperson
  • “11” for child dependent #1
  • “12” for child dependent #2
  • “29” for child dependent #19
  • “30” for vendor recipients

Note:  Payee code is now system generated and does not require the user to identify the appropriate code.

pendingConcurUserId

N/A

protectionIndicator

Shows “N” for all Chapter 33 claims.

participantBeneId

Shows a unique identifier for the specific participant who is the beneficiary of the transaction.

participantDepositAccountId

Identifies direct deposit payment address (required in participantPaymentAddrsID is blank).

participantPaymentAddrsId

Identifies physical address payment address (required in participantDepositAccountId is blank).

participantMailingAddrsId

Identifies the mailing address on file.

participantRecipientId

Identifies the recipient of the transaction (can either be the beneficiary or the school).

participantVetId

Identifies the Veteran used to establish eligibility.

remarksText

Null

receivableCollectionStatusTypeCd

Shows the collection status for negative values.

  • Defaults to “1” for beneficiary debts.
  • Defaults to “3” for vendor debts.

receivableTypeCd

Shows the type of receivable.

  • “71B” for beneficiary debts.
  • “75B” for vendor debts.

receivableReasonTypeCd

Required for all BTs with negative subsidiaryTranAmt.

  • “OTH” is used for reasons not otherwise identified.
  • “70” is used when an award period results in a negative BT.
  • “DB” is used for any negative awards related to the death of a beneficiary.

receivableRepaymentTypeCd

Shows “NULL” for all Chapter 33 claims.

receivableTransNumber

Shows the number of the BT when negative.

receivableId

Shows “NULL” for all Chapter 33 claims.

setupDate

Same value as the transactionDate.

setupUserName

Same value as the jrnUserID.

flexField3Text

Identifies the brand of service associated with the Veteran.

  • “AF” for Air Force
  • “AFR” for Air Force Reserve
  • “ANG” for Air National Guard
  • “ARMY” for Army
  • “AR” for Army Reserve
  • “ARNG” for Army National Guard
  • “CG” for Coast Guard
  • “CGR” for Coast Guard Reserve
  • “MC” for Marine Corps
  • “MCR” for Marine Corps Reserve
  • “NOAA” for National Oceanographic & Atmospheric Administration
  • “NAVY” for Navy
  • “NR” for Navy Reserve
  • “PHS” for Public Health Service
  • “SF” for Space Force

flexField4Text

Identifies the entitlement source.

  • “FRY” for Fry entitlement source.
  • “SELF” for Self entitlement source.
  • “TOE” for TOE entitlement source.

flexField5Text

Identifies the facility code associated with the BT.  Required for all BTs except Miscellaneous and Manual awards.
