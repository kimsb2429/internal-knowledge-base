 Duplicate Claim (combine duplicate file numbers)

Pending Issue Clear (clears pending EPC)

PIF

Pending Issue File (stored information in BDN while EPC is pending)

PINQ

Pending Issue Inquiry (reviews information in a pending EPC)

 Resume (activates suspended master record)
