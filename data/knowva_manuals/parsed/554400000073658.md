**8.01  INTRODUCTION**

---

Processing fiscal transactions have traditionally always been a function of Finance, however as business line boundaries blur, more and more fiscal transactions are being processed in Education. This chapter provides an explanation of the Benefits Delivery Network (BDN) screens used to process fiscal transactions, although not all may be done in Education. Some transactions remain exclusively Finance responsibilities and the information provided is strictly background information provided is stricktly background information.

*NOTE 2: Special Payments/One-time payments cover special situations in which money may be owed to, or needed by the veteran.  These transactions are independent of Award Processing.  The payment may or may not result in the establishment of a debt that the veteran must repay.  The following are the situations that the chapter 1607 Fiscals are designed to cover:*

**d. 06A One time:**   The 06A-One-Time releases a payment without affecting the master record other than updating the last activity field on the M21 screen.  No overpayment is created by this transaction.  This transaction is used only in those rare instances when it is necessary to bring an account to current status if the award lines are correct but the payee is due money.  Master record status must be “A” or “E” to process.

**e. 06G-Special Payment.** The 06G-Special Payment releases a payment and establishes a 55A accounts receivable. Payment is in expectation of an award.  Maximum amount of transaction allowed is $1,500.00.

**f. 06L Work-study Payment.**06L-Work-Study payment releases a payment predicated on the contractual work-study agreement. Maximum amount of transaction allowed is $1,100.00.

**g.  06T-TUTORIAL PAYMEN**T. The 06T-Tutorial Payment releases a payment and does not affect the Master Record as long as the total paid out has not reached $600.  Once the total has reached $600 the entitlement is charged.  The total amount paid for tutorial can not exceed $1,200.00.  Tutorial payment is only allowed for Institute of HIgher Learning/Non-College Degree (IHL/NCD).  The master record status must be “A” or “E” to process.

**h.  06B-ONE TIME PAYMENT-ESTABLISH A/R**.  The 06B-One Time Payment releases a payment and establishes or increases a “B” class receivable and will set the COLLECTION INDICATOR to a “3”. The master record status must be “A” or “E” to process.

**i.  EFT.**  If the Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) indicator is set to “Y” and the payee has a different address for this particular payment the “Y” can be changed to “N” and the check will be sent to the requested address. The EFT indicator in the master record will **NOT** be changed based on the Fiscal processing.

**j.  FRPT-FISCAL TRANSACTION REPORT**.  The fiscal transaction report will reflect added sections for chapter 1607 pending and authorized transactions.

**8.08   FOES GUIDE**

All fiscal information messages from the Hines system will be obtained using the Fiscal Notice of Expectations (FOES) command.  Mailing of hardcopy output is eliminated through the use of the financial message system.  It is imperative that these messages be obtained as the first item of business each day.  (In the event the system is unavailable, up to 3 workdays of FOES messages can be stored.)

**a.  Access FOES messages**.  Use of the FOES command will automatically print all messages on the station’s file.  If no FOES messages exist, a message will be displayed on the READY screen.  The following indicates the types of FOES messages and their origin.
