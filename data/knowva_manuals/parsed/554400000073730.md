# Chapter 2. Eligibility

**Chapter 2. Eligibility**

This section outlines who is entitled to receive the Post-9/11 GI Bill Educational Assistance benefit.

The Post-9/11 GI Bill Educational Assistance (Chapter 33) program offers education benefits for service members and veterans who have served on active duty for at least 90 days after September 10, 2001. Benefit levels are determined by the number of days served on active duty or qualifying active duty mobilization(s) from a Reserve or National Guard unit. Members are eligible for benefits 15 years after their last date of discharge and may receive up to 36 months of chapter 33 benefits.
