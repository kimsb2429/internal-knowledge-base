# Sub-Chapter 6. Service Data LTS Screen

**Sub-Chapter 6. Service Data LTS Screen**

**a. General**

The **Service Data** screen is used to add, edit, or update service data (including training or excluded service periods) to establish chapter 33 eligibility and entitlement.

If a record is associated with **VADIR (**VA/DoD Information Repository), service data will auto-fill. 

**![Chapter 33 Service Data Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII56_Service_Data_LTS_Screen_Img1.jpg)**

If no **VADIR** record is associated, the data will need to be entered manually. Click the **Add Service** button to continue. A pop-up window will appear.

 

**b. Add/Edit Service Period Pop-up Window**

---

The Add/Edit Service Period pop-up window allows you to add a service period not populated from VADIR. Generally, this is based on the receipt of a DD214 or contact with a service component.

Fields with an asterisk are required and include the following:

* Enter on Duty (EOD) Date\*
* Branch of Service (dropdown)\*
* Separation Reason (dropdown)\*
* Character of Service (dropdown)\*
* Release from Active Duty (RAD) Date
* Qualifying Service
* Active Duty (Yes or No)

Once the service information has been entered, click **Save** to continue.

**![Add/Edit Service Period](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII56_Service_Data_LTS_Screen_Img2.jpg)**

Once data from the Add/Edit pop-up window have been saved, the window will go away and you will be taken back to the main Service Data Screen.

**Comments** are required on the Service Data screen if any service data are entered manually or modified. An error message will be displayed as a reminder that comments are required.

When you have completed adding service data as needed, click the **Save** button on the Service Data Screen to continue to the **Entitlement and Kickers** screen.

The data will also be saved if you click on the **Previous** button.
