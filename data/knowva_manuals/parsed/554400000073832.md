# Sub-Chapter 7. Entitlement and Kickers LTS Screen

**Sub-Chapter 7. Entitlement and Kickers LTS Screen**

**a. General**

The Entitlement and Kickers Screen allows you to do the following:

* Add entitlement used under other VA education benefits,
* Indicate the benefit relinquished,
* Add Kickers and/or supplemental educational assistance benefits to which the claimant is entitled,
* Enter a CH33 Start Date,

Transfer entitlement to eligible dependents.

**![Entitlement and kickers Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII57_Entitlement_and_Kickers_LTS_Screen_Img1.jpg)**

Click the **Add Entitlement** button to access the **Add/Edit Entitlement Used** pop-up window**.**

 

**b. Add/Edit Entitlement Used Pop-up Window**

---

The Add/Edit Entitlement Used pop-up window allows you to select the **Type** of entitlement and enter the amount of **Entitlement Used**.

*In the example below, the claimant has used 16 months and 05 days of Chapter 30 entitlement. He is eligible at the time of application for CH33 benefits.*

**![Add/Edit Entitlement Used](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII57_Entitlement_and_Kickers_LTS_Screen_Img2.jpg)**

Click the **Save** button to continue. The **Entitlement Used** section is updated to include the previous entry. You can make another entry if necessary.

To make another entry, click the **Add Entitlement** button again to add a second benefit.

*In the example below, the claimant also has Chapter 1606 eligibility, although he has not used any entitlement.*

Click the **Save** button to return to the **Entitlement and Kickers** screen.

The **Entitlement Used** section now includes both entries. 

**![Entitlements Used](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII57_Entitlement_and_Kickers_LTS_Screen_Img3.jpg)**

Once you have completed adding entitlements, click the **Add Benefit Relinquished** button to activate the **Add/Edit Benefit Relinquished** pop-up window.

**c. Add/Edit Benefit Relinquished Pop-up Window**

---

In this pop-up window, enter data for any education benefit a claimant is relinquishing. Fill out the following:

* Enter the **Election Date**.
* Select the **Benefit Relinquished**.

If you are selecting **Chapter 30**, you must also enter the amount of months and days of remaining entitlement.

In the example below, the claimant is relinquishing **Chapter 30** benefits effective August 1, 2009, and he has 19 months and 25 days of remaining entitlement under **Chapter 30**.

**![Add\Edit Benefit Relinquished](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII57_Entitlement_and_Kickers_LTS_Screen_Img4.jpg)**

Click the **Save** button to continue.

[Back to Top](https://vaww.vrm.km.va.gov/system/web/widget/platform/spellchecker/spellchecker.html#_top)

 

**d. Add Kicker/Supplemental Educational Assistance Benefits**

---

To add a Kicker, click the **Add Kicker/Supplemental** button in the lower right corner of the main Entitlement and Kickers screen.

**![Add Kicker/Supplemental](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII57_Entitlement_and_Kickers_LTS_Screen_Img5.jpg)**

 

**e. Add/Edit Kicker/Supplemental Pop-up Window**

---

**![Add/Edit kicker/Supplemental example](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII57_Entitlement_and_Kickers_LTS_Screen_Img6.jpg)**

The **Add Kicker/Supplemental** pop-up window allows you to Add/Edit Kicker/Supplemental Educational Assistance eligibility. To do so, enter the following:

* The **Type** of benefit\*
* The full-time monthly rate (**$/month**)\*
* **Start Date\***
* **End Date** (if applicable)
* **Initial Entitlement** amount under **CH33\***

All fields with an asterisk are required.

*In our example, the claimant is eligible for a Chapter 30 kicker in the amount of $393.00 per month. His initial entitlement is the same as his remaining **Chapter 30** entitlement (19 months and 25 days).*

Click the **Save** button to continue.

The final results of entitlement and kicker entries are displayed on the Entitlement and Kickers screen.

Click the **Next** button to save the information and navigate to the **Enrollment** screen.
