# Sub-Chapter 10. Enrollment: Non-College Degree (NCD) - LTS Data Entry Rules

**Sub-Chapter 10. Enrollment: Non-College Degree (NCD) - LTS Data Entry Rules**

This section gives you step-by-step directions on how to add an NCD (Non-college degree) that is not from an institution of higher learning (IHL) to the enrollment screen in the LTS. This section will cover the following:

[10.02 Add Amendment](#1002)

**10.01 Add Non-College Degree Enrollment**

---

**a. General**

After the Entitlement and Kickers screen has been completed, you will navigate to the **Enrollment** screen. Click the **Add Enrollment** button to add a **NCD Non-IHL** enrollment.

**b. From the** **Add Enrollment** pop-up window, select the Training Type **Non-College Degree** from the drop-down box. This will display the **Add Non-College Degree Amendment (non-standard)** pop-up window. All fields with an asterisk are required.

**![this is the non college degree pop up, in the enrolment section of the long term solution system.](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII61001.jpg)**

**c. Enter the** **Begin Date,** which is the first day of the Term. In our example, we entered 10/01/2011 for the **Begin Date.**

**d. Enter the** **End Date** is the last day of term as shown on the enrollment certification. It is not the NO PAY DATE as used in other benefit systems. In our example, we entered 12/13/2011 for the **End Date.**

**e. Enter the 8-digit** **Facility Code.**

**f. Enter the 3-digit Program Code** as identified in WEAMS or M22-4. **Program Code and Program Name** is a required field and should be entered for each enrollment.

**g. Enter** **Approved Hours**. This field displays only when a **Non-IHL Facility Code** is entered in the **Facility Code** field.

Select the **Hour Type** from the following options:

* Clock (standard)
* Clock (non-standard)
* Only added to drop-down options when a Non-College Degree facility is entered (second digit 5, 6, 7, 8, or 9)
* Semester
* Quarter

In the example above, we selected **Semester**.

**h. Residence Hours** field is a required field. For this example, we entered 18 hours.

**i. Enter the** **Revised Res Hrs** (Residence Hours) and **Revised Dist Hrs** (Distance Hours).

**j. Tuition** and **Fees** are optional fields. In our example, we entered $1000 for **Tuition Charged.**

Click the **Save** button to save this enrollment.

**10.02 Add Amendment**

---

Once you have added an enrollment, the **Add Amendment** button will be enabled. If you want to make changes to an enrollment, click the yellow **Add Amendment** button.

**![this is an example of the long term solution enrollment scrren with the non college degree enrollment extended.](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII61002.jpg)**

In our example, we will add an amendment to reduce the **Res Hrs.**

**a. Click the Add Amendment** button, which allows the **Add Non-College Degree Amendment (non-standard)** pop-up window to appear.

Information from the original Enrollment will be available in this pop-up window as Read Only. Other fields will also require data:

* Effective Date
* Reason (drop-down box)
* Resume date
* Revised End Date
* Residence Hours
* Revised Res Hours
* Distance Hours
* Revised Dist Hours
* Tuition
* Fees
* Student Did Not Attend Class (checkbox)

**![This is an example of the pop up for adding an amendment to a non college degree enrollment.](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII61002a.jpg)**

**b. Enter the** **Effective Date** for the amendment.

**c. Select a** **Reason** from the Reason drop-down box, which includes these options:

* Increase
* Prereq Never Attended
* Reduction (NCD)
* Reduction NP
* Reduction Pun
* Reduction During Drop
* Completed NP
* End of Term or Course
* Graduation
* Unsat
* WD Before Term
* WD After Drop NP
* WD During Drop Pun
* WD During Drop
* WD or Interrupt (NCD)
* WD Before Term

For our example, we selected **Reduction (NCD)** from the drop-down box.

***NOTE:*** *The amendment **Reason** is separate from **MITC** reason, which will be captured in a different field. Increases or Decrease on the **First Day of Term** will be handled via a Correction in the system and will therefore not appear as an option in the Reason drop-down box. The **Amendment Reason** field is for data collection only and does not affect JRules.*

**d. The Resume Date** field is used when the VCE is showing a [Leave of Absence](https://eg5813aprl-int-nlb.egainstz.cloud/system/ws/v11/ss/article/554400000073850?portalId=554400000001018&usertype=customer&\$lang=en-us&). The Resume Date represents the date, after the leave of absence, on which the claimant resumes the enrolled hours before the Effective Date.

**e. Leave this field blank** if the amendment is not for a **Leave of Absence**.

**f. The** **Revised End Date** represents an extension of the enrollment as a result of a **Leave of Absence**. Please do not enter data into this field.

**g. The** **Revised Res Hrs** (Residence Hours) and **Revised Dist Hrs** (Distance Hours) fields are required. Enter 16 for revised **Res Hours** and leave the **Distance Hours** as 0.

**h. The** **Student Did Not Attend Class** checkbox should be checked if the claimant did not attend class for the hours being dropped. Do not select this box for our example.

**i. Click the** **Save** button to save the data, and return to the **Enrollment** screen.

**j. The** **Enrollment** screen displays the original Enrollment with the Amendment.

**![this is an example of the long term solution enrollment screen with the non college degree which has a amendment extended.](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII61002b.jpg)**

**10.03 Leave of Absence**

---

To enter a Leave of Absence to an enrollment, click the **Add Amendment** button to allow the **Add Non-College Degree Amendment (non-standard)** pop-up window to appear.

**a. Enter the** **Effective Date** for the Leave of Absence.

**b. Enter** **the** **Resume Date** to indicate the day on which the student resumed class.

**c. When** **Resume Date** is entered, the following occurs:

* The **Reason** value defaults to **WD or Interrupt (NCD).**
* The **Student Did Not Attend** checkbox is removed.
* The **Revised Res Hours** and **Revised Dist Hours** fields are disabled.

The **Revised End Date** represents an extension of the enrollment as a result of a Leave of Absence.
