# Sub-Chapter 11. Payment Instructions LTS Screen

**Sub-Chapter 11. Payment Instructions LTS Screen**

**a. Payment Instructions**

The **Payment Instructions** screen is mostly a read-only screen. You can view the status of transactions automatically sent to the BDN here. It summarizes the following:

* Debts
* Recurring Payments
* Other Monetary Benefits, such as stipends, allowances, or supplemental monies
* Miscellaneous Benefits

You may also enter any payment instructions if there is a need for the manual input of fiscal transactions into BDN [Hyperlink to system].  You will use the Comments box at the top of the screen for this purpose. Click **Save**. The claim is now complete.
