# Chapter 3. Training Programs

**Chapter 3. Training Programs**

The Post-9/11 GI Bill offers benefits for service members, veterans, and their dependents who are attending education and training programs at accredited colleges or universities, and through non-college degree (NCD) programs at other institutions (after October 1, 2011).

Use this table for a description of the programs covered under the Post-9/11 Education Assistance benefit and their requirements.
