# Sub-Chapter 3. Work Product History - LTS Data Entry Rules

**Sub-Chapter 3. Work Product History - LTS Data Entry Rules**

This section describes data entry rules for the Work Product History screen in the Long Term Solution (LTS) system.  You will find data entry rules to help you with the following user screens:

[3.01 Work Product History](#301)

[3.02 Entitlement Source](#302)

[3.03 Work Product Creation](#303)

[3.04 Multiple Work Products](#304)

 

**3.01 Work Product History**

---

**a. Work Products**

In many cases, a claimant will have multiple Work Products, as explained in the Multiple Work Products section below.

New claimants will not have any Work Products at this time. To add a Work Product, click the **Add Work Product** button on the right-hand side of the screen.

**![LTS Chapter 33 Add Work Product Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII63_Work_Prodiuct_History_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img1.jpg)**

The user will be taken to the **Entitlement Source** screen. 

**3.02 Entitlement Source**

---

**a. Entitlement Source**

Refers to the individual whose period(s) of service will be used to establish eligibility. On the Entitlement Source screen, the user selects the *source* for Chapter 33 benefit:

* Claimant’s own service (SELF)
* Fry Scholarship (FRY)
* Transfer of Entitlement (ToE)

Because a claimant can have more than one entitlement source to draw from, more than one may be listed. However, only one source may be selected and used in LTS. 

**![LTS Chapter 33 Entitlement Souce Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII63_Work_Prodiuct_History_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img2.jpg)**

 

Click the **Select** button next to the entitlement source to continue to the **Work Product Creation Screen.**

*\*\*For training purposes, we have selected SELF.*

**3.03 Work Product Creation**

---

**a. Work Product Creation Screen**

The user enters descriptive data for the claimant in two fields:

* Date Received
* Description

If this is the first time a claimant has filed for Chapter 33 benefits, the Work Product Type will be listed as an **Original Award**. 

If this is not a first-time claimant, it will be listed as **Supplemental Award.**

 

**b. Date Received**

In the Date Received field, enter the date the application was received.

**NOTE:** The "Date Received" field in DGI must be an accurate representation of the date of claim for the work being done, this date must match the end product (EP).

 

**c. Description**

Description refers to what the award is being used for, such as school term or semester. For the Description field, follow RPO standard **TIMS** [Hyperlink to TIMS] annotation procedures, as this will be displayed on the Work Product Summary screen.

**NOTE:** In the **Entitlement Source** area you will see the SSN, Name, and Type of entitlement listed as read-only.

 

**d. Create Work Product and Cancel buttons**

Once the Date Received and Description data have been entered, click on the **Create Work Product** button to save the data and navigate to the Service Data screen.

If you wish to cancel the data you entered, click the **Cancel** button.

**![LTS Chapter 33 Create New WP](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII63_Work_Prodiuct_History_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img3.jpg)**

**3.04 Multiple Work Products**

---

**a. Multiple Work Products**

In many cases, a claimant will have multiple Work Products. Once a claimant has filed for chapter 33 benefits, his or her file will show each award under the Work Product History, as illustrated below. 

Work Product History will include the following data:

* Date Received
* Work Product Source (i.e.: CH33)
* Work Product Type
* Claimant SSN
* Description
* Owner
* Status
* Date Authorized
* Authorized By

You will also see a hyperlink to either Edit or View a Work Product, as well as Edit buttons.

To review an Authorized Work Product, click the **View** link to navigate to the **Work Product Summary** screen.

To edit the In Progress Work Product, click the **Edit** button.

Only a Senior VCE can delete a Work Product with a status of *In Progress*. An *Authorized* Work Product cannot be deleted.

**![LTS Chapter 33 Claimant Source](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII63_Work_Prodiuct_History_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img4.jpg)**

In the example above, you can see that two of the Work Products have been authorized and one is In Progress.

* You can only “View” or “Edit” the Description of the Authorized Work Products.
* You can choose to “Continue” or “Edit” an In Progress Work Product.
* You can have only one Work Product with an *In Progress* status at a time. Thus, if a claimant has a Work Product with a status of *In Progress*, you cannot start another Work Product unless the current one is deleted or authorized.
