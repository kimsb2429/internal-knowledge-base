# Sub-Chapter 4. Service Data - LTS Data Entry Rules

**Sub-Chapter 4. Service Data - LTS Data Entry Rules**

 

This section describes data entry rules for the Service Data screen in the Long Term Solution (LTS) system. You will find rules to help you with the following:

[4.01 Service Data](#401)

[4.02 Add/Edit Service Period Pop-up Window](#402)

[4.04 Delete Service Data](#403)

 

**4.01 Service Data**

---

**a. Banner**

The Eligibility and Entitlement banner appears on the Service Data screen and all subsequent screens. This banner is for information purposes only and cannot be edited. It will be updated from data input on other screens.

The Service Data screen is used to complete the Work Product by adding, editing, or updating service data, training, and excluded periods to establish eligibility and entitlement.

If a record is associated with **VADIR** (VA /DoD Identity Repository), service data will auto-fill. If a record is associated with VADIR it cannot be deleted but may be edited. 

**![LTS Chapter 33 Service Data](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII64_Service_Data_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img1.jpg)**

If there is no VADIR record, you will have to enter data manually. Click the **Add Service** button to continue. A pop-up window will appear. 

**4.02 Add/Edit Service Period Pop-up Window**

---

**a**. **The Add/Edit Service Period** pop-up window allows you to add a service period not populated from VADIR. These are generally based on the receipt of a DD214 or contact with a service component.

Fields within the Add/Edit Service Period pop-up window include the following (fields with an asterisk are required):

* Enter on Duty (EOD) Date\*
* Release from Active Duty Date
* Branch of Service (dropdown)\*
* Separation Reason (dropdown)\*
* Character of Service (dropdown)\*
* Activation Statute
* Non-Qualifying

**![LTS Chapter 33 Service Entey Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII64_Service_Data_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img2.jpg)**

**b. Service Entry**

****The EOD Date** is auto-formatted as MM/DD/YYYY. For example, January 1, 2012 must be entered as 01/01/2012. The EOD date must not be before the claimant’s 16th birthday and cannot be greater than the current date.**

If applicable, enter the date the claimant was released from active duty in the **Release from Active Duty** field. This is also auto-formatted as MM/DD/YYYY.

Choose **Branch of Service** from the options in the dropdown list.

Choose **Separation Reason** from the options in the dropdown list.

Choose **Character of Service** from the options in the dropdown list.

Choose **Activation Statute** from the options dropdown list.

* Default is **N/A** and is used for Active Duty periods of service.
* **Title 10** option is Reserve or National Guard member performing duty under Title 10 orders and, unless noted, would qualify for CH33 eligibility and entitlement.
* **Title 32** option is for the following Army National Guard and Air National Guard members and, unless annotated, would qualify for CH33 eligibility and entitlement:
  + Those providing full-time service for the purpose of organizing, administering, recruiting, instructing, or training the National Guard;
  + Those activated under section 502(f) for the purpose of responding to a national emergency declared by the President and supported by Federal funds.

Once the service information has been entered, click **Save** to continue.

**![LTS Chapter 33 Display od Service History](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII64_Service_Data_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img3.jpg)**

After completing the Add/Edit Service Data fields, the Eligibility and Entitlement Banner is updated. The Service History section displays the information you entered.

**4.03 Delete Service Data**

---

**a. Remove Service Data**

If you need to delete service data, you can do so by selecting the checkbox next to the service period. By checking the box, you will enable the Delete Service Data button.

Click the **Delete** button to continue and return to the Service Data screen.

**![LTS Chapter 33 delete service history selection with comment](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII64_Service_Data_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img4.jpg)**

If any Service Data (including Training or Excluded periods) were manually added or modified, you must enter a **Comment** on the Service Data screen. If you do not, you will receive a validation error.

Service Data is the only screen where a comment is required.

Enter your comments in accordance with standard RPO annotation procedures. 

**![LKTS Chapter 33 Service Data Comments](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/XII64_Service_Data_LTS_Data_Entry_Rules_Img5.jpg)**

 

After Service Data has been completed, click **Next** to go to the Entitlement and Kickers screen.
