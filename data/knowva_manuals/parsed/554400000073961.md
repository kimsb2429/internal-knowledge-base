# Sub-Chapter 12 Approved Courses

**Chapter 33 Manual**

**Sub-Chapter 12. Approved Courses**

The Post-9/11 GI Bill was implemented on August 1, 2009. Approved training under the Post-9/11 GI Bill encompasses the following types of programs:

* Undergraduate, graduate, and professional degrees
* Vocational training
* Flight training
* On-the-Job and Apprenticeship training
* Correspondence training
* Licensing, certification, and national testing programs
* Entrepreneurship training
