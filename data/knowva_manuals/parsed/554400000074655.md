Veterans Benefits Administration Manual M22-3, "Manpower Control and Utilization in Education Activities," is changed as follows:

 

            Due to the extensive changes, brackets are not used to show changes.

 

            **Pages i through iv:**  Remove these pages and substitute pages i through iv attached.  The title of this manual has been simplified.  Page iii is reprinted without change.  Page iv is printed to update publications rescinded by this and previous changes.

 

            **Pages 5-i through 5-5:**  Insert pages 5-i through 5-5 attached.  This is a complete revision of the chapter on quantitative measurement.

 

            **Appendix A:**  Insert Appendix A attached.

 

            This new appendix replaces the former Appendix C.  It incorporates the program-specific authorization EPCs (end product codes) which are presently in use, as well as the definitions currently used for education services EPCs. 

EPC 215 is redefined for use by regional processing offices for special projects, and EPCs 820, 870, and 880 are reserved for future use.  600 series EPCs will no longer be used for education activities.  EPCs 910, 920, and 960 also will no longer be used for education activities.  Education activities formerly under the first two of those codes are now included in EPC 930, while those under the last code are now included in EPC 210.

**FOREWORD**

 

Generally, this manual has been confined to subject matter which is for specific application to Education activities in measuring performance.

 

The mission of the Education Service is to provide educational and training opportunities to eligible veterans and dependents, promote and assist the All-Volunteer Force and the Total Force Concept of the Armed Forces and encourage membership in units of the Selected Reserve and the National Guard.  It is important that the responsibility for this mission is carried out at the highest productivity level and quality level of which each responsible activity is capable.
