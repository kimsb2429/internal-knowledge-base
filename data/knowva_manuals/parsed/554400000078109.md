**8.01 Search for Claimant**

---

**a. Search**

You will start a claim for a Fry Scholarship recipient the same way you would for a regular claim: by searching Chapter 33 records and VADIR.

When you receive VA Form 22-5490, which indicates that a claimant is applying for Fry Scholarship, start by searching the LTS with the **SSN** of the claimant. Then click the **Search Chapter 33** button.

**![LTS Search](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image002.jpg)**

**b. No Results**

If no results are found in the Chapter 33 LTS, you will see the message *No Results Found.* Then a **Search VADIR** button becomes available on the far right of the screen.

**![LTS Search No Results](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image003.jpg)**

Continue the claim by searching the VADIR database. Click the **Search VADIR** button.

**c. No Record**

If no VADIR record is found for the claimant, click the **Create New Chapter 33 Record** button to continue the claim.

**![LTS Search No Record](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image004.jpg)**

In our example, no VADIR record is found for the claimant.

**8.02 Claimant Biography**

---

**a. The Claimant Biography screen** will be autofilled if a claimant’s record is in VADIR. If there is no record in VADIR, you will need to manually enter the claimant’s biographical information. 

On the Claimant Biography screen, the **Fry (Parent LOD) Date** cannot be edited and will be populated once the Original Work Product is authorized. Enter all the required data as you would with a regular claim.

**![LTS Claimant Biography Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image005.jpg)**

Click the **Next** button to save the record and continue the claim.

**8.03 Work Product History**

---

**a. Work Product History**

New claimants will not have any Work Products at this time. Click the **Add Work Product** button on the right-hand side of the screen.

**![LTS Work Product History](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image006.jpg)**

The user will be taken to the **Entitlement Source** screen.

**b. Entitlement Source**

**Entitlement Source** refers to the period of service from which the entitlement will be drawn.  On the Entitlement Source screen, the user selects the *source* for Chapter 33 benefit:

* Claimant’s own service (SELF)
* Fry Scholarship (FRY)
* Transfer of Entitlement (ToE), if applicable

**![LTS Entitlement Source Screen](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image007.jpg)**

​Choose **Fry** by clicking the **Select** button next to the **Fry** source. This will take you to the **Work Product Creation** screen.

**c. Work Product Creation**

There are 2 sections on this screen that need data entered:

* Create New Work Product
* Fry Scholarship

Enter all the required data for these sections. All fields with an asterisk are required. They are:

Data Received

* Description
* Parent First Name
* Parent Last Name
* Parent Line of Duty
* Parent Last Branch of Service

**Note:** **Parent Date of Death** is an optional field. 

**![LTS Create a New Work Product Screen - FRY](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image008.jpg)**

Click the **Create Work Product** button to continue.

**d. Service Data**

You will be brought to the **Service Data** screen. In our example, **Fry Scholar** is added under the claimant’s name in the top of the screen. The Eligibility and Entitlement banner is also updated. 

**![LTS - Member Service Data](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image009.jpg)**

The **Service Data** screen can be bypassed because the claimant is not a veteran. If the claimant had served on active duty, service data would be added, just as for an individual with **Self** as the **Entitlement Source.**

Click the **Next** button to continue to the **Entitlement and Kickers** screen.

**8.04 Entitlement and Kickers**

---

**a. Enter any Entitlement Used** under other (non-CH33) benefits. If the Fry Scholarship recipient is eligible for Chapter 30 (MGIB), Chapter 1606 (MGIB-SR), or Chapter 1607 (REAP), he or she must relinquish eligibility for one of these benefits. The individual may receive any Chapter 30 (MGIB), Chapter 1606 (MGIB-SR), or Chapter 1607 (REAP), kicker to which he or she is entitled under another benefit. 

**b. Eligible Under Own Service**

If the individual is eligible for Chapter 33 benefits based on his or her own service and is also eligible for the Fry Scholarship, the individual’s entitlement is NOT limited to a maximum of 36 months between those two Entitlement Sources. Only if the individual has used entitlement under other (non-CH33) benefits, the 48-month and or 81-month rules apply. **NOTE:** The current DGI, Chapter 33 Processing System design is able to manage only one chapter 33 source of entitlement. Proper processing of multiple sources relies upon workaround procedures. See: [Part 12, Sub-Chapter 3. Dependents](https://eg5813aprl-int-nlb.egainstz.cloud/system/ws/v11/ss/article/554400000073749?portalId=554400000001018&usertype=customer&\$lang=en-us&) 3.08 Fry Scholarship.

**c. Transferring Entitlement**

The Fry Scholarship participant may also be qualified to transfer entitlement to a dependent. If this is the case, entitlement is transferred out in the same manner as for an individual with **Self** as the **Entitlement Source.**

Click the **Next** button to save and continue to the **Enrollment** screen.

**8.05 Enrollment**

---

**a. The Enrollment screen** is where a school enrollment is added for a claimant.

On the Enrollment screen, click the **Add Enrollment** button to navigate to the Add Enrollment pop-up window. 

**![LTS Enrollment Screen - FRY](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image010.jpg)**

**b. Select the Training Type** from the drop-down list in the pop-up window, and continue with enrollment as you would with any other claim. Click **Save** to continue with the claim.

**8.07 Work Product Summary**

---

**a. The** **Work Product Summary** screen will give you a printable snapshot of all pertinent CH33 information. 

On the Work Product Summary screen, the **Fry Scholarship** section now displays the data from the **Work Product Creation** screen. 

Click the **Letter** icon to compile the LTS-generated **Education Award Letter.** The letter will be generated based on information from the LTS. Review the letter for accuracy. 

**![LTS Work Product Summary Screen - FRY](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image012.jpg)**

**Note**: You have the capability to edit the letter if necessary. In addition to an award for education benefits, the text may be edited to reflect changes to eligibility, entitlement, or enrollment status contained within the Work Product. 

**![LTS Work Product Screen with error - FRY](https://www.knowva.ebenefits.va.gov/img/edu/M22-4_Part_12_Chapter_8_image013.jpg)**

Because this is an **Original Work Product**, the claim will need to be sent to another VCE or SVCE for Authorization. After you have completed the data and reviewed your work, click **Done**.
