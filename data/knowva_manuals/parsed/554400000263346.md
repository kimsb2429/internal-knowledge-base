| **II.2.C.1.a**  **BT Generation** | DGI is programmed with rules that are used to determine and populate the necessary information to create both payments and receivables sent to eMPWR.  These rules consider a wide variety of information including eligibility determinations, enrollments and amendments entered on the Enrollment screen, and any manual or miscellaneous awards entered on the Work Product Summary (WPS) screen.  Only some of these calculated values are displayed on the DGI WPS and Payment Instruction (PI) screens.  The calculated details for a given business transaction (BT) can be viewed in the eMPWR system.    *Reference:* See Appendix B in [Appendices](https://eg5813aprl-int-nlb.egainstz.cloud/system/ws/v11/ss/article/554400000263373?portalId=554400000001018&usertype=customer&\$lang=en-us&)for full details on the possible values.  See [M22-4, Part II, 2.E](https://eg5813aprl-int-nlb.egainstz.cloud/system/ws/v11/ss/article/554400000263354?portalId=554400000001018&usertype=customer&\$lang=en-us&)  for details on how to review BTs in eMPWR. |

| **II.2.C.1.b**  **Transmission of BTs to eMPWR** | Upon the authorization of a work product that generates payments and/or receivables in DGI, BTs are immediately transmitted to eMPWR with receivables being sent first.  Payments due in the future are stored by the system until they mature (or are adjusted by interim work).  Upon maturation and enrollment verification (where applicable; see [M22-4, Part XII, 11](https://eg5813aprl-int-nlb.egainstz.cloud/system/ws/v11/ss/article/554400000191366?portalId=554400000001018&usertype=customer&\$lang=en-us&) for details), *payments are transmitted to eMPWR without additional Veteran Claims Examiner (VCE) action.*    Unlike BDN, BTs will generally be transmitted to eMPWR immediately upon authorization.  Any transactions submitted after 5:59:59 PM CT will be processed by eMPWR on the following business day. Matured BTs are visible in eMPWR nearly immediately upon authorization, though payment details take one business day. |

| **II.2.C.1.c**  **Notifying DMC** | If a receivable exists or a new receivable is generated upon the authorization of a work product, DGI will send a notification of all scheduled housing payments to the Debt Management Center (DMC). The notification(s) will provide the appropriate data needed for DMC collections.    *No VCE action is needed regarding this issue.* |

| **II.2.C.1.d**  **Debt Offsets** | Some payments can be used to offset existing debts during the debt collection process.  Automatic debt collection abides by the following rules.   * “Recurring” beneficiary payables are protected from offset. * “Retro” books and supplies (71B) payables can be directly offset against any 71B receivables with the same veteran/ beneficiary relationship, a matching award type code, and congruent dates between the payable and the receivable.  No action on the collection indicator need be taken. * “Retro” housing and kicker payables can be directly offset against either housing or kicker receivables under specific circumstances.   Appliable to any receivable in a “collectible” status that does not have a BR established (i.e., no active repayment plan).   * “Retro” payables for a Vendor (75B) can be offset for any Vendor receivable with the same veteran/ beneficiary relationship, a matching award type code, and congruent dates between the payable and the receivable.     *No VCE action is needed regarding this issue.* |
