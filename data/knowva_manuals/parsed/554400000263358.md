| **II.2.F.1.a**  **General** | DGI can automatically process many original applications and subsequent enrollments submitted by a beneficiary or their associated institution using an automation engine.    The system opens a work product in DGI and runs through a series of automation rules and checks for every potentially automated claim that is received.  If no rules are triggered, the claim will be authorized and any payments due or letters needed are released.  If a rule is triggered, the claim is offrampedand a token is created in The Image Management System (TIMS) for Veteran Claims Examiner (VCE) review. |
